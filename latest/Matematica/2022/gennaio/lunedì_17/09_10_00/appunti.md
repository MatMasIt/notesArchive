# Esercizi di mantematica

$$
\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{e^x-e^{2x}}{\ln(1+3x)}=\frac{1-1}0=\frac00\quad FI
$$

$$
\xrightarrow[\ln(1+3x)\approx 3x]{e^x\approx x+1,e^{2z}\approx 2x+1}
$$

$$
\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{x+1-2x-1}{3x}=-\frac{x}{3x}=-\frac13
$$

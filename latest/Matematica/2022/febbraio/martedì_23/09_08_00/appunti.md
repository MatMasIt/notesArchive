## De l'Hopital


$$
f,g \in D(I)\quad I\subset D,I(x_0)
$$

Prequisiti
1.
$$\lim_{x\to x_0}f(x)=\lim_{x\to x_0}g(x)=0$$
$$
\lim_{x\to x_0}f(x)=\lim_{x\to x_0}g(x)=\pm \infin
$$
2.
$$
g(x)\neq 0 \quad \forall x \in I(x_0)
$$
3.
$$
\exists \lim_{x\to x_0}\frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)}
$$

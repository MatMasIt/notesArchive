Moto uniformemente accellerato
$s=s_{0}+v_{0}t+\frac12at^2$
$v(t)=\frac{ds(t)}{dt}=v_{0}+\frac{1}{\cancel{2}}a\cancel{2}t=v_{0}+at$
$a(t)=\frac{dv(t)}{d(t)}=a$

$\frac{d^2s(t)}{dt^2}=a(t)=\frac{dv(t)}{dt}=a$
